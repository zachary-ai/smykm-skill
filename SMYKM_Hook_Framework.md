# SMYKM Hook Framework
## Research ‚Üí Subject Line ‚Üí Opening Hook System

---

## üéØ WHAT THIS IS

A systematic approach to crafting personalised subject lines and opening hooks that demonstrate you've done your homework on the prospect. The rest of the email depends on your client's value proposition.

**Output:**
1. Compelling subject line (under 60 characters)
2. Personalised opening hook (1-2 sentences)
3. Natural bridge to your client's value proposition

**Time Investment:** 5-10 minutes per prospect

---

## üîç RESEARCH PROCESS

### 5-Minute Research Sprint

1. **LinkedIn Check (2 min)**
   - Recent posts (last 30 days)
   - Job changes/promotions
   - Personal achievements mentioned

2. **Company Quick Scan (2 min)**
   - Company age (calculate from founding year)
   - Recent news on website
   - Awards or recognition

3. **Google Search (1 min)**
   - "[Company name] + news" (past 3 months)
   - "[Person name] + [Company]"

### Research Priority Matrix

| Priority | Type | Examples | Why It Works |
|----------|------|----------|--------------|
| **Level 1** | Company Milestones | Anniversaries (25, 50, 100 years), IPO, Acquisition | Universal relevance, celebration angle |
| **Level 2** | Personal Achievements | New role, award won, challenge completed | Shows individual focus |
| **Level 3** | Recent Company News | Expansion, new product, partnership | Timely and relevant |
| **Level 4** | Personal Interests | Marathon, music, charity work | Creates human connection |
| **Level 5** | Industry Context | Market trends, regulatory changes | Fallback option |

### Research Capture Template
```
Company: [Name]
Person: [Name, Title]
Hook Found: [What specific thing]
Date/Recency: [When did this happen]
Source: [Where you found it]
Connection Angle: [How this could relate to any B2B solution]
```

## ‚úâÔ∏è SUBJECT LINE FORMULAS

### Formula 1: Achievement Recognition
**Structure:** [Number/Achievement] + [Reaction]
- "50 years - impressive milestone"
- "3 awards in one quarter!"
- "$100M funding - huge congrats"

### Formula 2: Curiosity Question
**Structure:** [Observation] + ?
- "Berlin expansion next?"
- "500 employees already?"
- "IPO in 2025?"

### Formula 3: Personal + Professional
**Structure:** [Personal thing] + [Professional role]
- "Marathon runner AND CEO"
- "Author of 3 books while scaling"
- "Jazz drummer building fintech"

### Formula 4: Company + Context
**Structure:** [Company] + [Timely observation]
- "Acme Corp crushing the ASX"
- "TechCo's rapid expansion"
- "[Company] making waves in [industry]"

### Formula 5: Specific Callout
**Structure:** [Specific thing you noticed]
- "Your LinkedIn post on resilience"
- "The Sydney office opening"
- "Your team's award win"

## üé£ OPENING HOOK TEMPLATES

### Milestone Hook
```
Noticed [Company] just celebrated [milestone] - [specific observation about what this means].
```
Examples:
- "Noticed Acme just hit 25 years - that's a quarter-century of innovation in a tough market."
- "Saw TechCo crossed 500 employees - that kind of growth brings exciting challenges."

### Achievement Hook
```
[Specific achievement] - [acknowledgment of difficulty/impressiveness].
```
Examples:
- "Completing an Ironman while running a hypergrowth startup - that's serious discipline."
- "Three industry awards in one year tells me you're doing something very right."

### News/Announcement Hook
```
Saw the news about [specific event] - [insight about what this means for them].
```
Examples:
- "Saw the news about your Berlin expansion - entering the EU market is a bold move."
- "Your recent Series B announcement caught my attention - scaling from here is where it gets interesting."

### Personal Interest Hook
```
[Personal interest] + [business connection] = [observation].
```
Examples:
- "Jazz musician turned CEO - I bet that improvisation skill serves you well in this market."
- "Your post about climbing Kilimanjaro reminded me that the best leaders embrace difficult climbs."

### Industry Context Hook
```
With [industry change], I imagine [Company] is [likely situation].
```
Examples:
- "With the new regulations hitting financial services, I imagine compliance is top of mind."
- "As manufacturing reshores to Australia, your timing couldn't be better."

## üåâ BRIDGE STATEMENTS

These connect your hook to ANY client's value proposition:

### Universal Bridges
- "That's exactly the kind of [challenge/opportunity] where we help companies..."
- "In situations like this, we typically see companies need..."
- "This kind of [growth/change/milestone] is where..."
- "Companies at this stage often tell us..."
- "Leaders dealing with [situation] frequently need..."

### Growth Bridges
- "That level of growth usually means..."
- "Scaling at this pace requires..."
- "Companies growing this fast often..."

### Challenge Bridges
- "Navigating [challenge] successfully requires..."
- "Companies facing [situation] typically need..."
- "This kind of transition is where..."

## ‚ö° QUICK EXECUTION GUIDE

### Step 1: Research (5 min)
1. Open LinkedIn, company website, Google News
2. Find ONE strong hook (don't overthink)
3. Fill in research template
4. Note the date/source for accuracy

### Step 2: Subject Line (1 min)
1. Pick appropriate formula
2. Keep under 60 characters
3. Make it specific, not generic
4. Don't use client company name (save for email)

### Step 3: Opening Hook (2 min)
1. Select matching template
2. Insert specific details
3. Keep to 1-2 sentences max
4. End with connection to their situation

### Step 4: Bridge (1 min)
1. Choose universal bridge
2. Connect to client's value prop
3. Make transition feel natural

## ‚úÖ QUALITY CHECKLIST

**Research:**
- [ ] Information is from last 90 days (ideal) or still relevant
- [ ] Hook is specific, not generic
- [ ] You have the source if questioned

**Subject Line:**
- [ ] Under 60 characters
- [ ] Specific reference included
- [ ] Would make YOU open it
- [ ] No spelling errors

**Opening Hook:**
- [ ] Shows you did homework
- [ ] Feels natural, not forced
- [ ] 1-2 sentences maximum
- [ ] No assumptions or exaggerations

**Overall:**
- [ ] Took less than 10 minutes
- [ ] You'd be happy receiving this
- [ ] Clear path to value prop

---

## üö´ AVOID THESE MISTAKES

1. **Generic Observations:** "I see you're in financial services"
2. **Outdated Information:** Using news from 2+ years ago
3. **Creepy Details:** Personal family information
4. **Multiple Hooks:** Trying to cram 3 achievements into one opening
5. **Fake Familiarity:** "I've been following your company for years"
6. **Weak Connections:** Forcing irrelevant link to your solution
7. **Being Wrong:** Not fact-checking your research
8. **Copy-Paste Feel:** Using exact same structure every time

## üé™ ADAPTING FOR DIFFERENT SENDERS

### CEO/Founder Sending
- Reference shared experiences
- Peer-to-peer tone
- "Building [company], I've learned..."

### Sales Rep Sending
- Focus on insight over introduction
- Reference pattern recognition
- "Working with companies like yours..."

### Consultant Sending
- Lead with expertise
- Industry-wide perspective
- "Across the [industry], we see..."
